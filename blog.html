12th November 2024– Responding to the growing demand for faster and more affordable delivery options, Ru Motors is excited to announce the launch of its new motorcycle delivery service. Designed for urban businesses and individual customers, this innovative service promises a quicker, more reliable alternative to traditional delivery methods, meeting the increasing need for on-demand convenience. With our streamlined fleet of motorcycles, we offer the flexibility to navigate traffic, access tight spaces, an packages at record speed. Local businesses—especially restaurants, retail shops, and e-commerce providers—can benefit from competitive rates, fast delivery times, and lower operational costs.

“We saw a gap in the market for a delivery service that combines affordability, agility, and reliability,” said Jacin Thiong’o. “By utilizing motorcycles, we not only cut down on delivery times but also reduce our carbon footprint, providing a greener choice for businesses and their customers.”

Rush Motors’ delivery model also allows users to track their packages in real-time via our app, ensuring transparency from dispatch to drop-off. Our riders are professionally trained, insured, and committed to safety, reinforcing our dedication to reliable service.

As an introductory offer, new business clients can enjoy a 10% discount on their first month’s deliveries. Rush Motors invites local businesses to learn more about how this service can support their operations.
